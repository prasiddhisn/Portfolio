College Attendance Management System

Introduction

The College Attendance Management System is an indispensable tool designed to streamline the process of tracking student attendance within institutions. It offers functionalities for insertion, deletion, and modification of daily attendance data, allowing faculty members to maintain precise attendance records and monitor absenteeism efficiently.

Features

  • Unique login details for every user
  • Insertion, deletion, and modification of student attendance details
  • Viewing of student attendance details
  • Add new students and teachers
  • Change passwords

Users and their accessible features

Admin: Login, add, modify and delete student attendance, view student and class attendance, change passwords, add teachers and students.

Teacher: Login, add, modify and delete student attendance, view student and class attendance, change passwords, add students.

Student: Login, view attendance, change passwords.
